Discover the person behind the Accent

I'm Dimitris, a hospitality professional with international experience across the UK, Greece, Malta, and beyond. Passionate about guest experience and digital transformation, I bring a unique voice to the industry.

Hotelier with an Accent is a podcast born from curiosity, creativity, and the desire to speak honestly about how hospitality is changing. It covers everything from operations to leadership and technology — always with a touch of personality, and of course, an accent.
